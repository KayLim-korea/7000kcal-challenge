Q: How do I connect Apple Health?

A: Open the app and tap "Connect Health Data" on the home screen. Allow access when prompted. You can also manage permissions in Settings > Privacy > Health.

Q: Why isn't my calorie data syncing?

A: Make sure Apple Health permissions are granted. Try pulling down to refresh on the home screen. Check that your Apple Watch is synced with your iPhone.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?

A: Go to iPhone Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions > 7000 Kcal Challenge > Cancel Subscription.

Q: How do I delete my account?

A: Go to Profile tab > Settings > Delete Account. Your data will be removed within 30 days.

Q: What is Active Energy vs Total Energy?

A: Active Energy is the calories you burn through movement and exercise. Total Energy includes your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR). Our app tracks Active Energy only.
