7000 Kcal Challenge ("App") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

  • Email address (for account creation and login)
  • Display name/nickname
  • Profile photo (optional)

b. Health Data (Apple Health Integration)

HealthKit Data Usage
The App integrates with Apple Health (HealthKit) to read the following data:
  • Active Energy Burned
  • Step Count
  • Heart Rate
This data is stored on Google Firebase servers to track your calorie challenge progress and enable social features (friend competitions). Data is encrypted in transit and only accessible by you and users you explicitly allow through friend features.

c. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • App usage data
  • Push notification tokens

2. Advertising and Tracking

The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. For personalized ads:

  • We may use IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers)
  • The App requests tracking permission on first launch
  • Declining tracking does not limit app functionality (non-personalized ads will be shown)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide calorie burn challenge services
  • Enable Battle/Raid game features
  • Track progress and provide statistics
  • Send push notifications
  • Respond to customer inquiries
  • Improve services and develop new features

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Retention Period: Until account deletion
  • Deletion: All personal data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
  • Data may be retained longer if required by law

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who assist in app operations
  • Legal authorities when required by law

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Data storage and authentication
  • Google AdMob: Advertising services
  • RevenueCat: Subscription payment management
  • Apple: Payment processing (for in-app purchases)

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Opt-out of ad tracking (Settings > Privacy > Tracking)

Exercise these rights through the app settings or by contacting us.

8. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Encryption of personal data
  • Access control management
  • Regular security updates
  • Firebase security rules

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 14. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 14. If we discover such data, it will be deleted immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be notified through the App.

11. Contact Us

Privacy Inquiries
